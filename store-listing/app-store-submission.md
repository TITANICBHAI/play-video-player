**Full Body Text Description (min 50 words):**
```
Play is a free, privacy-first video player for Android that puts you in full
control. No ads, no accounts, no tracking — ever. Play works entirely offline,
scanning your device automatically to build a clean media library.

Key Features:
- Smart gesture controls: swipe left/right for brightness and volume, double-tap
  to seek ±10 seconds, pinch to zoom between Fit and Fill modes
- External subtitle support (.srt) with automatic persistence per video
- Resume playback — remembers where you left off in every video
- Playback speed control: 0.5x to 2.0x
- Picture-in-Picture (PiP) and background audio playback
- Chromecast and AirPlay support for casting to your TV
- Scriptable Smart Sort — filter and group your library with custom JavaScript
  expressions or ChatGPT-generated templates
- Technical Stats Panel — view live resolution, bitrate, file size, and hardware
  decoding status right inside the player

Play is built for people who want a beautiful, modern, and completely private
video experience on their local files — with no compromises.
```

**Description:**
```
Play is a free, privacy-first video player that gives you a premium experience
on your local files — no ads, no accounts, no tracking.

Automatically scans your device for videos and builds a clean media library.
Smart gesture controls let you swipe for brightness and volume, double-tap to
seek, and pinch to zoom. Load external .srt subtitles with automatic persistence.
Resume playback from where you left off, cast to Chromecast or AirPlay, and use
```
